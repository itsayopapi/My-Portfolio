About Us

Welcome to Amala T-Square Spot, the home of the best Amala and Abula in Surulere, Ijesha, Lagos. For over a decade, we have been serving delicious, authentic Nigerian meals that satisfy both local and international customers.

Our story began in 2010 when Mama Titi, our head chef, decided to share her family's secret recipes with the community. What started as a small roadside spot has now grown into one of Lagos' most beloved eateries.

Our meals are prepared with the freshest ingredients, following traditional recipes and cooking methods that bring out the rich flavors of Nigerian cuisine. We take pride in our meticulous preparation process that ensures every dish meets our high standards.

Whether you're dining in or taking out, we guarantee a memorable culinary experience that will keep you coming back for more.
